Prateik Sinha

Machine Learning Engineer @ Dassault Systemes
Researcher @ The MINT Group & Roychowdhury Group at UCLA

Hello! My name is Prateik Sinha, I am a machine learning engineer at Dassault Systemes. I work on 3D computer vision and natural language processing for SolidWorks.

I'm researching AI for atmospheric sciences with Professor Aditya Grover and AI for neuroscience with Professor Vwani Roychowdhury at UCLA. My research interests range from AI for Science to interpretability, computer vision, foundation models, and generative AI.

I recently graduated from UCLA with majors in Mathematics of Computation and Statistics and Data Science. During this time I interned as a ML engineer at Dassault Systemes and as a data scientist at Zelis. I also served as the projects director of ACM AI at UCLA.
